Customer Service Representative

Responsibilities

  • Responsible for growing sales by partnering with the franchise owner and providing outstanding customer service to all Crafts Emporium customers through in-depth knowledge of company products and services.
  • Acts as the first point of contact for new and existing customers to establish a rapport and build confidence in Crafts Emporium services.
  • Educates customer on terminology, policies and procedures, anticipated delays and any additional information needed to increase customer satisfaction and improve profitability.
  • Prepares all daily move-related paperwork and documents, including strong computer skills.
  • Obtains customer feedback through regular follow-up calls, both during and after moves to ensure ongoing customer satisfaction.
  • Developing new marketing-sales initiatives with the franchise owner to increase brand awareness in the community.
  • Participates in various marketing-sales activities including networking, direct mail and charitable move coordination.

Qualifications

  • A minimum of six months to one year of customer sales and/or customer service experience is required.
  • High school diploma or general education degree is required.
  • Strong team player working productively with a range of effective selling and technical strategies.
  • Excellent customer service skills and effective listening and communication skills.

Package Handler

Responsibilities

  • Receive and put away inventory
  • Get customer orders ready and pack them up
  • Load boxes into trucks for shipment
  • Work around moving machines like order pickers, forklifts, mobile carts, and possibly robots!
  • Exchange safety tips daily with co-workers
  • Use scanners to scan bar codes on products
  • View prompts on screens and follow directions for some tasks

Qualifications

  • Lift up to 22 kilograms / 49 pounds
  • Stand, walk, push, pull, squat, bend, and reach during long shifts
  • Use carts, dollies, hand trucks, and other gear to move stuff around
  • If you want to and there is a need for it, you can get trained to use powered industrial trucks
  • Go up and down stairs (for sites with stairs)
  • Work at a height of up to 12 meters / 40 feet on a mezzanine (for some sites)
